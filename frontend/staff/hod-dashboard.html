📌 How to Approve/Reject
  1. Review: Check the student's leave application and advisor's decision
  2. Verify: Ensure it aligns with departmental policies
  3. Action: Click "Approve" or "Reject" button
  4. Remarks: Optionally add comments (required for rejection)
  5. Track: Once Host approves → Final Status = APPROVED
🔄 Approval Workflow

Your Role: Head of Department (HOD)

You are the SECOND level of approval. You review after advisor's decision.

Workflow:

1️⃣ Advisor → Already approved

2️⃣ You (HOD) → Approve/Reject

3️⃣ Host → Final approval

✅ All approve → Leave APPROVED
